# Web3 BD Portfolio

Welcome to my professional GitHub portfolio, demonstrating 5+ years of expertise as a Senior Business Development Manager in Web3. This repository showcases analyses, case studies, and tools focused on tokenomics, Fully Diluted Valuation (FDV), market fit, Initial DEX Offerings (IDOs), Venture Capital (VC) networks, launchpads, smart contract audits, and automated workflow pipelines.

Key themes:
- **Tokenomics & FDV**: In-depth reviews of supply mechanics, vesting schedules, and valuation frameworks to ensure sustainable growth.
- **Market Fit & IDOs**: Evaluations of project alignment with DeFi, AI, and community dynamics, including successful launch strategies.
- **VCs & Launchpads**: Network mappings and partnership strategies with Tier-1 entities like a16z Crypto, Binance Labs, Seedify, and Polkastarter.
- **Audits & Compliance**: Checklists and templates for due diligence, emphasizing security and regulatory alignment.
- **Workflow Pipelines**: Python-based scripts for deal sourcing, CRM integration, and pipeline management.
- **Event Insights**: Key takeaways from Tier-1 conferences like Consensus, NFT NYC, and Devcon, highlighting AI-DeFi intersections.

This portfolio supports my track record of sourcing $50M+ in IDO deals and building ecosystems in AI and DeFi. All content is original or based on public data for educational purposes.

## Featured Projects
- **AI-Focused**: Fetch.ai, SingularityNET – Tokenomics analyses showing AI utility in decentralized networks.
- **DeFi-Focused**: Aave, Uniswap – FDV and market fit evaluations for lending and DEX protocols.
- **Hybrid**: Ocean Protocol – IDO case study blending AI data markets with DeFi liquidity.
