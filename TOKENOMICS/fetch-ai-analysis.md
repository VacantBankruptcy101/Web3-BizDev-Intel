- **Total Supply**: 1.15B FET
- **FDV**: ~$2.5B (as of Nov 2025)

**Strengths**:

**BD Recommendations**: Partner with launchpads like ChainGPT for AI-focused IDOs, targeting $500M+ ecosystems.
